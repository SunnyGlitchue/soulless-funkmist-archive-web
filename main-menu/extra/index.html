Funkmist Facts

  1. Until 2.0 development, Bootleg Spongebob and Yummer we're planned to have weeks, until 2024, when Fabs decided to remove each week's two songs and leave one song for each of the for Freeplay.
  2. In Mid 2024, old Soulless Funkmist dev servers were wiped out, resulting in most of the old Soulless Funkmist art and music as lost media.
  3. The mod was cancelled due to Fabs, the director, not liking the mod anymore.
  4. In Squirrel Jokes, it was meant to incluide Dev OCs in the background, this idea was briefly scrapped.
  5. BNRAN was renamed to Killer Scary Single Squidward with Patrick Killed Over the Floor Song due to the original name's meaning, the devs didn't wanted to get in trouble, so they asked other devs to suggest renames, Sunny suggested the name we all know today.
